ABOUT MAVINS

Mavin Records is a Nigerian music corporation record label founded by record producer and recording artist Don Jazzy on 8 May 2012. The labels's inception came to fruition following the closure of Mo' Hits Records, a record label owned by the aforementioned producer and D'banj.
